About Us

Established in 1968, M/s. Chandulal J. Parikh has gained immense expertise in Production & Marketing of Agriculture seeds of multiple varieties like Groundnut, Pearl Millet, Wheat, Castor, Fennel, Mustard, Cumin, Sorghum, Gram, Green Gram, Black Gram, Psyllium Husk, and Sesame. The company is located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, and is one of the leading sellers of listed products.
